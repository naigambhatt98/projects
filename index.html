Creating a Personal AI Financial Assistant

Reflections on design, iteration, and generative AI

Why I Chose This Project

I’ve always been drawn to practical applications of AI. When given the chance to create something that blended utility with creativity, I wanted to focus on a problem people face every day. Personal finance felt like the right space to explore.

The goal was to use generative AI not just for rapid prototyping but to guide thoughtful experience design. Canva’s generative site builder enabled me to iterate quickly and shape ideas into a working user flow.

Visuals & Process

I used Canva’s generative AI features to design an interactive prototype of a personal financial assistant. Rather than starting with raw code, I mapped out the user journey visually. Explore it here: View Financial Assistant Site.

I began with a prompt: "Design a homepage for a financial planning assistant with clean layout, bold section titles, and input forms." Canva offered several variants, and I refined the ones with strong CTA and visual clarity—eventually leading to this third iteration.

Design Thinking and Generative AI

This wasn’t about building another webpage—it was about building trust. I asked myself: What would someone need from an assistant when they're overwhelmed with finances? The answers: clarity, simplicity, and a sense of control.

With AI-assisted tools, I could iterate rapidly. I crafted each module as a friendly guide, making sure the tone felt helpful, not robotic.

"Generative AI let me focus on intent over interface. I could act as both a designer and a decision-maker."

What I Learned

This experience showed me that the best AI tools don’t replace thought—they accelerate it. Canva’s interface reduced busywork and freed up space for real UX thinking.

I also learned the value of modular design. Each assistant tool—budgeting, saving, investing—was its own component, making the process clearer and output cleaner.

Conclusion

My financial assistant prototype is a small glimpse of what’s possible when thoughtful design meets powerful AI tools. If it can reduce someone's anxiety around money, it's done its job.
