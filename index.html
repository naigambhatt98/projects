Bio

I’m Naigam Bhatt, a robotics engineer with a Master’s degree in Mechatronics, Robotics, and Automation Engineering from NYU. With nearly three years of experience working on real-time 3D perception pipelines, multi-camera robotics, and GPU-accelerated vision systems, I specialize in blending creative problem solving with technical rigor.

I have built complete pipelines that support 60Hz real-time robotic mapping using multiple 3D cameras, developed efficient CUDA kernels for geometric algorithms, and contributed to research published in ISMR 2023.

Value Proposition

I bring a deep understanding of both software and hardware in robotics, enabling me to design holistic systems. I focus on building reliable, real-time, and efficient perception pipelines that scale, while making design decisions that balance performance, clarity, and usability.
